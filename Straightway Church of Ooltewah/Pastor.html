What Are The Ice Cream Chronicles?

Life can get “heavy” at times. We created ICC as a fun, light-hearted way of inviting newcomers to church. We hope you’ll catch our short episodes each Saturday evening as Pastor James (with an ice cream cone in hand) extends his personal invitation to you!
